ESQUIRES SPRING DRINKS

CHERRY BACKWELL LATTE

  1. TWO shots of espresso(Regular)/ONE shot of espresso(Small)
  2. ONE pump of cherry syrup
  3. ONE pump of chocolate syrup
  4. ONE pump of amaretto syrup
  5. Steam/stretch milk to 140°F/60°C
  6. Tap and swirl milk
  7. Pour milk and create latte art
  8. Serve on saucer with teaspoon

CHERRY MOCHA

  1. TWO shots of espresso(Regular)/ONE shot of espresso(Small)
  2. ONE HEAPED scoop of 30% choc powder
  3. THREE pumps of cherry syrup
  4. Steam/stretch milk to 140°F/60°C
  5. Tap and swirl milk
  6. Add vanilla vegan whip cream
  7. Dust of chocolate powder
  8. Serve on saucer with teaspoon

CHERRY CHOCOLATE SHAKE

  1. FULL glass of ice into jug
  2. Milk to 300ml line
  3. THREE pumps of liquid frappé
  4. ONE HEAPED scoop of 30% choc powde
  5. THREE pumps of cherry syrup
  6. Add vegan vanilla whip
  7. Dust of choc powder
  8. Serve in tall glass with straw
